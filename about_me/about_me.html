My Favorite trip

I recently visited Barcelona and was fascinated by its mix of history and modernity. I walked along Las Ramblas, where the atmosphere was lively and full of energy, with street performers, markets, and cafés everywhere.

One of the places that impressed me the most was the Sagrada Familia. Gaudí’s architecture is simply stunning. I spent a while admiring the details on the façade and then went inside to see the colorful stained glass lighting up the interior.

I also really enjoyed the food. I tried delicious tapas and had a paella by the sea in Barceloneta. The weather was perfect, so I spent the afternoon walking along the beach and watching the sunset.

My Favorite Restaurant

One of my favorite places to eat is Boqueria Restaurant in Boston, MA. I love it because the dishes are creative and full of flavor, and the atmosphere is elegant yet welcoming.

Paella – My Favorite Dish

I absolutely love paella, especially when it's full of fresh seafood. I like to serve it hot with a slice of lemon on the side and enjoy it with friends or family. The mix of rice, saffron, and seafood flavors is just perfect. It's a dish that always reminds me of summer and celebration.
